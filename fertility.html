What's the success rate of IVF at your clinic?

+
Our clinic maintains a 72% success rate for women under 35, significantly higher than the national average. We achieve 65% for ages 35-37, 55% for 38-40, and 40% for 41-42. Each case is unique, and we provide personalized success projections during consultations.

How long does the IVF process take from start to finish?

+
A complete IVF cycle typically takes 4-6 weeks: 10-14 days for ovarian stimulation, 1 day for egg retrieval, 3-5 days for embryo culture, and the transfer. Additional time may be needed for pre-treatment testing (2-4 weeks) and frozen embryo transfers (3-6 weeks).

What tests are required before starting treatment?

+
  • For women: Hormone tests (AMH, FSH, LH), ultrasound scan, HSG
  • For men: Semen analysis, DNA fragmentation test
  • For both: Infectious disease screening, genetic testing

What's the difference between IUI and IVF?

+

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination): Sperm is washed and placed directly in the uterus during ovulation (success rate 10-20% per cycle).

IVF (In Vitro Fertilization): Eggs are retrieved, fertilized in lab, and embryos transferred (success rate 40-70% per cycle). IVF is more effective but more invasive and expensive.

Is there an age limit for fertility treatments?

+
We treat women up to age 50 using their own eggs, and up to age 55 with donor eggs. Success rates decline significantly after 40. For men, there's no strict age limit but sperm quality typically declines after 45.
