Hitaishi Hospital - Ear, Nose & Throat Care

Expert care for your ear, nose, and throat problems

Ear Care

We diagnose and treat various ear conditions such as:

  • ✔Hearing loss and age-related hearing decline
  • ✔Ear infections (acute & chronic otitis media)
  • ✔Tinnitus (ringing in the ears)
  • ✔Balance disorders & vertigo (BPPV, Meniere’s disease)
  • ✔Impacted earwax removal

Our audiology department offers:

  • ✔Advanced audiometry and hearing evaluations
  • ✔Digital hearing aid fitting & programming
  • ✔Custom ear protection for noise and water

Nose Care

Our ENT specialists provide comprehensive care for conditions like:

  • ✔Allergic rhinitis and seasonal allergies
  • ✔Acute and chronic sinusitis
  • ✔Nasal polyps and deviated nasal septum
  • ✔Nosebleeds (epistaxis) and nasal trauma
  • ✔Obstructive breathing and snoring

We also offer:

  • ✔Endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS)
  • ✔Image-guided nasal procedures
  • ✔Nasal endoscopy for diagnosis

Throat Care

We offer expert diagnosis and treatment for:

  • ✔Tonsillitis and pharyngitis (sore throat)
  • ✔Vocal cord nodules, polyps, and hoarseness
  • ✔Sleep apnea and chronic snoring
  • ✔Swallowing difficulties (dysphagia)
  • ✔Throat tumors and infections

Our services include:

  • ✔Speech therapy for voice rehabilitation
  • ✔Polysomnography (sleep studies)
  • ✔Minimally invasive throat surgeries
